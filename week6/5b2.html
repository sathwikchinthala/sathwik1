About Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amrita University) is a top-ranked, NAAC A++ accredited private deemed university in India established by Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma). It has 10+ campuses (Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Kochi, Amritapuri, Chennai, etc.), offering 300+ programs in engineering, medicine, sciences, and humanities for over 30,000 students. It is highly regarded for research, sustainability, and value-based education..
